Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about orders, shipping, returns, and more.

What is your shipping policy?

+

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50 within the continental US. Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for $9.99. International shipping rates vary by destination. Orders typically process within 1-2 business days before shipping.

How do I track my order?

+

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and link. You can also track your order by logging into your TechSphere account and visiting the "Order History" section. If you have any issues with tracking, contact our support team.

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items in their original condition with packaging. Some items like opened software or personalized products may not be eligible. To start a return, visit our Returns Center in your account or contact customer service for a return authorization.

Do you offer price matching?

+

Yes, we offer price matching on identical products from authorized retailers. The item must be in stock at the competitor at the time of the request. Price matches must be requested within 7 days of purchase. Contact our customer service team with the competitor's details for assistance.

How do I check warranty status?

+

Most products come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty. You can check your warranty status by logging into your account and viewing your purchase history, or by contacting our support team with your order number. Extended warranty options are available for purchase at checkout.
